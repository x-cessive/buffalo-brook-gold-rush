## About the Game

Buffalo Brook Gold Rush is a realistic gold panning simulation that captures the excitement and challenge of the 1849 California Gold Rush. Players experience authentic panning mechanics, manage resources, and navigate a dynamic economy while searching for fortune.

### Key Features

- **Realistic Panning Physics** - Experience authentic sediment separation
- **Dynamic Economy** - Market prices fluctuate based on supply and demand
- **Tool Progression** - Unlock and upgrade equipment for better yields
- **Environmental Systems** - Weather and time affect gameplay
- **Cross-Platform** - Play on Windows, Linux, or Android

## Game Systems Overview

### Core Mechanics
The game revolves around gold panning, a process of separating gold from sediment using water and gravity. Players must master timing, technique, and tool selection to maximize their yields.

### Progression
Players progress by:
- Collecting gold through panning
- Purchasing better tools
- Unlocking new locations
- Upgrading equipment
- Building wealth

### Economy
A dynamic economic system simulates market forces:
- Gold prices fluctuate
- Supply and demand affect values
- Tools have costs and maintenance needs
- Strategic timing increases profits
