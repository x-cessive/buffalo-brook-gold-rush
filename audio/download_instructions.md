## Recommended Download Process

Step 1: Create a free account on Freesound.org
Step 2: For each sound listed above:
   a. Search using the provided search terms
   b. Check the license (prefer CC0 or CC BY licenses)
   c. Download the highest quality version available
   d. Save to the appropriate subdirectory in your audio folder
   e. Verify the file is named correctly

Step 3: For ambient loops:
   - Look for files that are specifically marked as "loopable"
   - Check if they have smooth start/end points
   - Test that they can be seamlessly looped

Step 4: Attribution Requirements:
   - For CC BY licensed sounds: Include attribution as specified by the creator
   - For CC0 licensed sounds: No attribution required but often appreciated
   - Keep a list of all sounds used with their creators and license info

## Alternative Free Sources

1. YouTube Audio Library (https://www.youtube.com/audiolibrary)
   - Completely free with no attribution required for most sounds
   - Good for ambient sounds and basic effects
   - Browse: Audio Library -> Sound Effects

2. Zapsplat.com (Free Section)
   - Attribution required
   - High-quality sound effects
   - Good for foley sounds

3. Open Game Art (https://opengameart.org/)
   - Game-specific sounds
   - Often under CC0 or similar licenses

## Quality Guidelines

- Download sounds in the highest quality available (ideally 44.1kHz/16-bit or higher)
- For loops, ensure they are seamless (no audible start/end clicks)
- For short effects, look for high-quality recordings with minimal background noise
- Prefer sounds that don't contain copyrighted music or voices
