## Quick Start Demo

### 5-Minute Gameplay Experience

This quick demo will introduce you to the core mechanics:

1. **Launch the Game** (2 minutes)
   ```bash
   # From Godot Editor
   Press F5 to run

   # Or from command line
   godot --path /path/to/Buffalo-Brook-Gold-Rush
   ```

2. **Main Menu**
   - Click "Start Mining" to begin
   - The game loads into the main gameplay scene

3. **First Panning Experience** (3 minutes)
   - Press `E` when prompted to start panning
   - Use `J` and `K` to tilt the pan left and right
   - Press `Spacebar` to shake the pan
   - Watch the sediment separate from gold
   - Complete the minigame to collect gold

4. **Check Your Results**
   - View your gold count in the HUD (top-left corner)
   - See your current tool displayed

## Feature Showcase

### 1. Gold Panning Mechanics

**What to Experience:**
- Realistic sediment separation physics
- Dynamic particle behavior based on tilt and shake actions
- Gold detection algorithm that rewards skillful play

**How to Demo:**
1. Start a panning action by pressing `E`
2. Observe the initial sediment amount
3. Tilt the pan (`J`/`K`) to see sediment slide out
4. Shake the pan (`Space`) to separate lighter materials
5. Watch as gold becomes visible through effective separation

**Key Observations:**
- Gold particles are heavier and settle at the bottom
- Sediment gradually reduces with proper technique
- Water amount affects particle movement
- Success depends on separation effectiveness

### 2. Tool System

**What to Experience:**
- Multiple tool types with different effectiveness ratings
- Tool durability that decreases with use
- Visual feedback on current tool

**How to Demo:**
1. Check the current tool display (HUD top-left)
2. Note the tool effectiveness during panning
3. (In full game) Purchase upgrades from the shop
4. Compare results with different tools

**Available Tools:**
- **Basic Pan**: Starting tool (1.0x effectiveness)
- **Gold Pan**: Improved efficiency (1.3x effectiveness)
- **Professional Pan**: Advanced tool (1.6x effectiveness)
- **Sluice Box**: High-capacity processing (2.0x effectiveness)

### 3. Dynamic Economy

**What to Experience:**

**How to Demo:**

**Economic Factors:**
- Base gold price with random fluctuations

### 4. Environment & Weather

**What to Experience:**
- Dynamic time of day system

**How to Demo:**

## Gameplay Loop

### Complete Session Walkthrough (15 minutes)

#### Phase 1: Getting Started (2 min)
1. Launch game and click "Start Mining"
2. Read the on-screen instructions
3. Familiarize yourself with the controls

#### Phase 2: Learning to Pan (5 min)
1. Position yourself near water
2. Press `E` to start panning
3. Practice the panning controls:
   - Tilt left with `J`
   - Tilt right with `K`
   - Shake with `Spacebar`
4. Complete 3-5 panning attempts
5. Develop a feel for the mechanics

#### Phase 3: Building Skills (5 min)
1. Experiment with different techniques:
   - Slow, controlled tilts
   - Aggressive shaking
   - Water management
2. Observe which methods yield more gold
3. Try to maximize each panning session

#### Phase 4: Progression (3 min)
1. Track your total gold accumulation
2. (In full game) Visit the shop
3. Consider tool upgrades
4. Plan your strategy for continued play

## Advanced Techniques

### Mastering the Pan

**Technique 1: The Gentle Swirl**
- Use subtle tilts (`J` then `K`)
- Allow sediment to gradually wash out
- Preserve gold particles at the pan bottom
- Best for high-sediment situations

**Technique 2: The Aggressive Shake**
- Rapid `Spacebar` presses
- Quick separation of light materials
- Faster but potentially less accurate
- Best for time-sensitive scenarios

**Technique 3: The Balanced Approach**
- Alternate between tilting and shaking
- Monitor sediment levels constantly
- Adjust technique based on remaining material
- Best for consistent gold yields

### Optimal Panning Strategy

1. **Assessment Phase**
   - Check initial sediment amount
   - Note weather conditions
   - Consider tool effectiveness

2. **Separation Phase**
   - Start with gentle tilts
   - Progress to controlled shakes
   - Watch for gold visibility

3. **Collection Phase**
   - Minimize excessive movement
   - Preserve discovered gold
   - Complete minigame carefully

4. **Result Analysis**
   - Review gold collected
   - Note success rate
   - Adjust technique for next attempt
